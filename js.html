BRAND NAME

Jwalini Retexturising Skin Treatment Oil

Overview

Prescribed in Ayurveda as a skin texture treatment for smooth, soft, and clear skin, this oil treatment is formulated from natural herbs processed in pure Coconut milk and Sesame oil. It helps clear up skin irritation and itching and is an excellent after-sun remedy for sunburn, tanned, or hyper-pigmentation. It contains Costus and Cardamom, known for their 'Varneekarana' properties - the ability to enhance skin texture and treat irritation. Regular use results in healthier, softer skin that is visibly smoother and radiant

How to use?

Take an appropriate amount onto palms and massage gently into skin until well-absorbed. Leave on for 30 minutes and wash off with a mild cleanser.
