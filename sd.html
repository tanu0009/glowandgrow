What it is:

The Mughal queens in the 16th century grew gardens of roses as they found this exotic bloom to have exceptional magical properties that they could use in their daily bath and beauty rituals. Sadhev’s Rose Water is a sweet fusion of handpicked centifolia roses and the essence of a 200 year old ancestral wisdom steamed, distilled and bottled.

What it includes

. Helps hydrate the skin Helps smoothen the skin Helps tones the skin Rejuvenates tired skin & eyes Natural & Ayurvedic

How to use?

Indian Rose petals contain sugars and natural oils that trap moisture in the skin, making the skin smooth and baby soft. Sadhev’s Rose Water is made with exotic centifolia roses, carefully harvested during the seasons of early winter to late spring when the pink buds start to show colour but the petals are yet to unfurl.
