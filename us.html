BRAND NAME

Deep Cleanse Facial Ubtan

Overview

Deep Cleanse Facial Ubtan is a traditional scrub, mask & cleanser that uses Ayurvedic ingredients like Neem, Papaya & Sandalwood to gently exfoliate & nourish the skin. The versatile formula promises to retain your natural glow.

What it includes

The ubtan multitasks as a cleanser, scrub, and mask. Draws out excess oil and impurities by polar attraction Purifies, nourishes, moisturizes, and soothes the skin Gently exfoliates skin

How to use?

SStep 01 Take equal parts of ubtan and water in a bowl. Step 02 Mix them until a paste-like consistency is achieved. Step 03 Gently massage over the face and neck and rinse after a few minutes or leave on the skin as a mask for 10-20 minutes Please note that for dry skin, it is advised to mix the ubtan with milk or milk cream instead of water
