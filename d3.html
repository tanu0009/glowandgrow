What it does

The scalp scrub removes buildup from the scalp caused by pollution, dirt, and excess oil production by gently exfoliating. It also deeply nourishes the scalp.

How to use?

Step 1: Apply the required amount of the scrub to the wet scalp. Step 2: Massage it for a couple of minutes in gentle motions. Step 3: Wash thoroughly with water and follow it up with a gentle shampoo. Use once or twice per week.
