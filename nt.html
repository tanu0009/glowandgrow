BRAND NAME

Rose Mist

Overview

This lightweight skin essence is made with our iconic Kumkumadi Oil, an Ayurvedic formulation of precious herbs that brightens dull skin and enhances radiance. Pure Kashmiri Saffron adds a dewy, golden glow while Liquorice brightens and helps remove excess melanin. With anti-inflammatory and antibacterial properties, Aloe Vera Leaf Juice present in this essence for skin soothes and heals. Plant-based emollients smooth out skin texture lending a subtle sheen. The combination of these powerful ingredients, packed in a bi-phase essence for face, helps prep skin and achieve a natural glow

What it includes

Ayurveda-inspired Skin Essence For Dewy, Golden Glow Kumkumadi Brightening Bi-Phase Essence is a unique two-phase formula infused with our iconic Kumkumadi Thailam and water-based actives that lock in moisture and lend a natural glow. Preps & Hydrates Skin The facial essence has Vitamin E, a powerful antioxidant that hydrates & strengthens the skin's natural barrier. Non-greasy & Lightweight Essence For Face This non-greasy bi-phase skin care essence doesn't leave any sticky residue making it easy to apply or add to the existing regime.

How to use?

Use A.M. and P.M. Shake well just before use to mix the two phases. Spritz onto face & neck, post cleansing & toning. Gently pat onto skin. Follow with a sunscreen/ night cream or moisturiser. Or use on-the-go anytime when needed.
