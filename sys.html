What does the Some By Mi Yuja Niacin Anti-Blemish Serum do?

The Some By Mi Yuja Niacin Anti-Blemish Serum combines the power of natural Yuja extract and Niacinamide to deliver a potent and transformative skincare experience. Specially formulated to target blemishes, breakouts, and acne scars, the Some By Mi Yuja Niacin Serum fades dark spots and promotes a clear, even skin tone. Derived from the vibrant Yuja fruit, this extract is a rich source of Vitamin C, known for its brightening properties. It helps to fade away dark spots, hyperpigmentation, and acne scars, revealing a more even and luminous complexion...

How to use?

After toner, apply a proper amount on the face and pat for better absorption
