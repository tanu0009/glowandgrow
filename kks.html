Overview

WHAT IS KUMKUMADI OIL? Kumkumadi oil, a traditional Ayurvedic formulation, is known for its various benefits for the skin. The key ingredient that makes Kumkumadi Oil is “Kesar” or “Saffron”. Kesar, also known as “Kumkuma” in Sanskrit, is the reason behind the name Kumkumadi. The 100% pure Kumkumadi Oil from Shankara India features a classic formulation made using Red Gold Saffron and other traditional Ayurvedic ingredients like Manjishtha and Red Sandalwood. This formulation is crafted by hand-stirring these ingredients and other natural ingredients like Sveta Chandana, Patranga, Raktachandana, Jati, Usira, etc., over a slow flame for several days. As a result, this natural micro-emulsion helps promote the skin's overall well-being. Made using clean ingredients and free from dyes, sulphates and synthetic fragrances, the oil is suitable for all skin types, particularly dry and rough vata skin. By penetrating deep into the layers, the Kumkumadi face oil benefits the skin by moisturizing and nourishing it and reducing fine lines, thus imparting a natural and youthful glow. Below mentioned are some of the Kumkumadi Oil benefits, that will convince you to include this miraculous elixir in your skincare routine.
