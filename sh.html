How to use?

Blend 2-3 teaspoons into a paste. Use Kama Ayurveda's Pure Rose Water/Pure Mogra Water (dry skin); or Pure Lavender Water/Pure Vetiver Water (sensitive, oily, acne-prone skin). You may also use Yoghurt, Milk, Aloe Vera or Honey, if you intuitively feel they work better for you. Apply to moist skin. Leave on until the paste has dried and rinse clean. Suvarna can be used once or twice a week. Different skin types react differently to a Natural product. Please conduct a patch test before application..
