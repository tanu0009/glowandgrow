BRAND NAME

Red SandalWood face pack

Overview

The Forest Essentials Chandan Varnya Lepa is a nourishing facial masque infused with pure Sandalwood paste, Saffron and Kokum Butter which effectively tone, firm and hydrate the skin. This is blended with pure steam distilled Rosewater, Nagkesar herb extract and Wheat Germ Oil extract to ensure a creamy rich consistency.

What it includes

Tones and firms the skin. Deeply hydrates and rejuvenates. Leaves the skin with a smooth porcelain finish

How to use?

Step 1: Cleanse your face and open the pores with a hot towel or steam for a few minutes. Step 2: Apply the Lepa all over the face and neck, avoiding the eye area. Step 3: Leave it till the paste is semi-dry. Step 4: Rinse well with cold water. Step 5: Use a chilled toner to finish
